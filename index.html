Instructions to play this version of black jack

  1. click Start Game
  2. You will get 3 options.HIT,STAND,DEAL
  3. HIT will pick a card from the pack
  4. your card value follows the regular black jack rules.
  5. You can pick as many cards you want using HIT but ensure that your sum of card values wont exceed 21. Then you are busted as per regular blackjack
  6. Once you click 'STAND' button the computer/bot picks its cards
  7. Click on DEAL to start picking the cards again
